Admission Overview

Age Criteria for Nursery

Children must be at least 3 years old by the start of the academic year to be eligible for Nursery.

Academic Criteria

Prospective students will undergo an aptitude or entrance test, which will determine their eligibility for admission. For Classes I to IV, the tests are designed to assess the knowledge gained in the previous year, primarily in English and Math, along with an evaluation of general awareness. From Class V onwards, the assessments will cover English, Math, and Science based on the skills acquired in the preceding class.

Credentials Required

Parents must submit the birth certificate, Transfer Certificate (If applicable), Copy of Aadhar Card, previous school report card (if applicable), and passport-size photographs.

Code of Conduct

For Students

  • Respect your peers, teachers, and all school staff.
  • Follow the school's rules and regulations.
  • Maintain a positive attitude and contribute to a harmonious school environment.
  • Be responsible for your own learning and actions.
  • Report any incidents of bullying or misconduct immediately.
  • Attend classes regularly and arrive on time.
  • Take care of school property and the environment.

For Parents

  • Support your child's education by ensuring they attend school regularly.
  • Communicate openly with teachers and school staff.
  • Encourage positive behavior and respect for others.
  • Be involved in school activities and support school policies.
  • Ensure your child completes homework and other assignments on time.
  • Respect the school's rules and cooperate with staff to create a conducive learning environment.
  • Report any concerns regarding your child's behavior or academic performance.
