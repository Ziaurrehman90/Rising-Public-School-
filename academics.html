Our Curriculum

Our school follows the English medium syllabus with maximum numbers of NCERT published books as prescribed by both U.P. & C.B.S.E. boards syllabus.
We focus on nurturing creativity, emotional well-being, and social skills, laying a strong foundation for future learning.

  • Focus on language, motor skills, and emotional intelligence
  • Personalized attention and joyful learning environment
