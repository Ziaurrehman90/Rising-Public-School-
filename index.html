Welcome to Rising Public School

Our school offers a unique environment that promotes holistic development through academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. With state-of-the-art facilities, modern methods for teaching, experienced & dedicated staff, we ensure that every student is empowered to reach their full potential.

Join us in shaping the future, where every student is nurtured, guided, and prepared to face the world with confidence and compassion.

Vision

Our vision is to empower students to be critical thinkers, compassionate citizens and life long learners in an ever- changing world. Develop a culture of respect and responsibility to lead a meaningful life.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that balances intellectual, emotional & social development, preparing students to thrive in a rapidly changing world. Also to inspire a love for learning & encourage students to persue their passions with purpose integrity & compassion.
